# Networking Fundamentals

> *"A strong understanding of networking is the foundation of every successful IT and cybersecurity professional."*

## About This Section

Networking is at the core of modern IT infrastructure. Every device, application, cloud service, and security solution relies on networks to communicate efficiently and securely.

Completing the CompTIA Network+ certification strengthened my understanding of networking fundamentals and helped me connect theoretical concepts with real-world scenarios that I encounter during my Service Desk operations on a daily basis. It allowed me to better understand how enterprise networks function, how devices communicate, and how structured troubleshooting leads to faster problem resolution.

This section serves as my personal knowledge base where I document networking concepts, practical examples, troubleshooting approaches, and their relevance to cybersecurity.

## My Learning

Studying Network+ completely changed the way I approach technical problems.

Many technologies I worked with in Service Desk—such as VPN, Active Directory, DNS, remote access, cloud services, and endpoint management—started making much more sense once I understood the networking concepts behind them.

Rather than memorizing protocols or commands, I learned to think logically about how devices communicate and where communication can fail.

---

## Cybersecurity Perspective

Every cyberattack travels through a network.

Whether it's phishing, malware communication, lateral movement, privilege escalation, or denial-of-service attacks, understanding networking fundamentals helps security professionals detect, investigate, and respond to incidents more effectively.

Networking remains one of the strongest foundations for building a successful career as a SOC Analyst.
